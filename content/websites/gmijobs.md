GMI Jobs ("Gonna Make It") aggregates live roles directly from the Applicant Tracking Systems of 200+ verified blockchain companies — including Coinbase, Uniswap, Chainlink, Aave, Arbitrum, Optimism, and more — via direct Lever, Greenhouse, Ashby, and Comeet integrations. Every listing is AI-enriched with day-to-day responsibilities, must-have skills, and company context. Features include ecosystem filters (Ethereum, Solana, Cosmos, etc.), salary data upfront, and hiring velocity badges.
