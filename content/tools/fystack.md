Fystack gives businesses the infrastructure to automate digital asset operations securely using MPC (Multi-Party Computation), at any scale. It provides wallet creation, transaction automation, multi-approval flows, spending policies, and audit logs through one unified API. Supports Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, TRON, BNB Chain, Base, and 10+ other networks.
